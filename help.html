Frequently Asked Questions

What time is check-in and check-out?

Check-in is at 4:00 PM and check-out is at 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out may be available upon request, depending on availability.

Is the property pet-friendly?

Yes! We welcome well-behaved pets. Please let us know in advance if you're bringing a furry friend, and note that a pet fee may apply.

What amenities are included?

Cloud Nine features a fully equipped kitchen, WiFi, smart TV, washer/dryer, ocean views, beach access, and outdoor seating areas. Check the listing for a complete list.

What is the cancellation policy?

We offer flexible cancellation options. Please review the cancellation policy on our booking page or contact us directly for specific details about your reservation.

How do I get directions to the property?

Detailed directions and access instructions will be sent to you after your booking is confirmed, typically 24-48 hours before your check-in date.
